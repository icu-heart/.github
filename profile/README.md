## 👋 About Us

We are a multidisciplinary academic research group working at the intersection of **critical care**, **cardiovascular science**, and **health data science**. Our work spans clinical data infrastructure, predictive modelling, and translational research, with the goal of improving patient outcomes in intensive care settings across Scotland and beyond.

We believe in **open, reproducible research** and collaborative building. We anticipate that the repositories within this GitHub organisation will be make publically available and open-source where possible.
